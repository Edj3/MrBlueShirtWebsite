Mr. Blue Shirt

Played by Michael Stewart


Mike loves being in front of the camera as much as being behind it as no doubt he is the "bell cow", so you will a heavy dose of Mike to no one's surprise in the Mr. Blue Shirt movies. before on-camera acting, he began with haunted house acting in the late 1990's as a young boy, then in 2002 at age 16 he got his first camcorder and has not looked back since that time. Mike studied at Northampton Community College and Kutztown Univeristy where he met many people who have helped bring Mr. Blue Shirt to life in the early test films of 2008 and 2010 and then with the regular MBS films. Mike hopes the different acting philosophies, funky editing style, and the strange dialogue brings about a huge fanbase to the fans of movies around the country... and who knows maybe even the world.


Blue is the supposed savior or messiah figure in the land of Ogidni. He does not exactly understand his purpose in the beginning, but as time rolls by; it becomes more clear: he is the main protagonist of Ogidni and Earth.

Mr. Violet Shirt

Played by Ben Lewis


Ben Lewis has been into acting and music since a very young age. Ben met Mike in 2004 at Northampton Community College and they made a short together on gay rights called, "Taste the Rainbow." Ben is one of the few lone survivors from the early test films of Mr. Blue Shirt during the years of 2007-2010 to make it to the modern day and "real Mr. Blue Shirt" movies as Mike Stewart so calls them.


Violet is the serious and no-jokes kind of a personality Shirt. In his time in Ogidni, he is blessed by King Haran to be the leader of the Shirts in the expedition to find Mr. Blue Shirt.

Zeromon

Played by EJ Mann


EJ is passionate about music, technology, fitness. Both pianist and programmer, EJ has been playing the keys and synth for almost 10 years. Also, EJ is not only the developer of the Mr. Blue Shirt site, but has programmed many of his own games and mobile apps as well. He enjoys parkour, running, and lifting. EJ participated on a showing of MTV's Silent Library along with Stew and a few of his friends during the show's very first season back in 2009.


Like Gandalf, Zeromon is the extra set of ears, eyes, and nose of high wisdom and experience that the Shirts, King Haran, and others of the Ogidini land don't have. Zeromon has a powerful wizard staff and mild telekinesis ability. He enjoys some fresh whiskey and cooked rat meats, not to mention an enthusiasts of potions and spells.
